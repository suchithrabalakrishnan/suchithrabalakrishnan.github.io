About Me

I am a Data Science and Analytics Leader with over eight years of experience building and deploying machine learning models, data pipelines, and analytics solutions across financial services and insurance. I have worked on a range of high-stakes problems from fraud detection and Anti-Money Laundering compliance to customer retention modelling and cybersecurity risk.

In my current role as Senior Manager at Fidelity, I work across multiple business units, including Enterprise Infrastructure, Anti-Money Laundering, Fraud Detection, and Cybersecurity, delivering data science solutions where they are needed most. I also mentor junior data scientists and interns, and have partnered with Boston University for three consecutive years to lead Fidelity-sponsored capstone projects on LLM evaluation and RAG system quality.

I pride myself on attention to detail, not just in the data itself, but in understanding what the business actually needs before jumping to a solution. I care about rigorous, reproducible work and models that are well-documented, well-tested, and trusted by the teams that use them.
